Papaya Salad

Juicy papaya tossed with sauce, cherry tomatos, and lime

This delicious side dish will have your family coming back for seconds!

Papaya salad, also known as "som tam" in Thai, is a refreshing and spicy salad made with shredded unripe papaya, tomatoes, carrots, peanuts, garlic, chili peppers, lime juice, fish sauce, and palm sugar. The ingredients are mixed together in a mortar and pestle, which allows the flavors to meld together and creates a slightly crunchy texture.

Ingredients

  • 5 Cherry Tomatoes
  • 2 chili peppers
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons vegan fish sauce or soy sauce
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 6 green beans
  • 2 cups shredded green papaya
  • 3/4 lime
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons date paste or sugar
  • 1/2 tsp tamarind paste
  • 2 green onions sliced
  • 1 handful cilantro ; chopped
  • 2 tablespoons toasted peanuts

Recipe Instructions

  1. Peel or shred papaya using grater, when close to center, discard any seeds

  2. Smash a clove of garlic in a bowl, then add green beans and halved cherry tomatos. Pound a few times just to bruise the beans and get the juice out of the tomatos.

  3. Add chili peppers and crush them just enough to release the hotness.

  4. Add the green papaya and toasted peanuts.

  5. Mix vegan fish or soy sauce, lime juice and date paste or sugar separately and add as needed.
